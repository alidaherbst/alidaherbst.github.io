Vision
We will increase our market share and fulfil our mission by creating a highly engaged team that is focused on our clients while providing value for our services.

Mission
To make renewable energy readily available and affordable to all, and to introduce alternative technologies and products to South Africa.

“We have learnt through visible evidence the power that renewable energy can have upon the lives of people. We can create light, hot water and energy from the sun. We can even create water and provide tools to add nutrients back to the soil. It is not equitable that as a company we create a barrier of access for common people to emancipate themselves from daily burdens that can be resolved through the application of our products. It is our responsibility to spread far and wide, to drive down prices and create products that allow every person to live a more efficient, more sustainable life.”

“We’re killing people in foreign lands to extract 200m year-old sunlight – we pollute our rivers, blow up mountaintops – for an hour of electricity, when we could just take what is falling free from the sky.”

Danny Kennedy

 Our main objective is simple: to make renewable energy readily available and affordable to all, and to introduce alternative technologies and products to South Africa.
