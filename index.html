Introduction 

Pecunua trading as Allsolar Pretoria East is a renewable energy company offering innovative energy solutions.

The goal of Allsolar Pretoria East is to make us all more energy conscious, thus reducing our impact on the planet. Allsolar Pretoria East aims to do this in a practical and affordable way. Saving energy will also save you money. Allsolar Pretoria East has access a wide array of tried and tested energy saving and energy generating products that will help cut your energy costs. Energy costs in South Africa are soaring out of control and we need to be aware of this. Take action now and limit the future financial impact on you. Team members of the Allsolar Pretoria East group have installed an inverter and 4 batteries at their house and has had awesome experience during load shedding no noisy generator, seamless change over to the batteries during load shedding and no petrol cost and maintenance costs for generators.

The services Allsolar Pretoria East offers are:

  • Energy Audit - Free Energy Consumption Audits.
  • Products – Energy Generation & Energy Efficiency.
  • Analysis – Efficient Energy requirements.
  • Design – Complete system design and engineering
  • Installation – Turnkey installation and commissioning.
  • Our business delivers innovative energy solutions on time and gives free energy audits and advice or input when requested.
